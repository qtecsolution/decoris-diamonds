NATURAL

Laboratory-grown diamonds are a great option if you are looking for an affordable alternative to natural diamonds without compromising on brilliance and quality. Created through an advanced technological process designed to mimic natural conditions, lab-grown diamonds are chemically identical to mined diamonds. Remember, the grading method used for natural diamonds also applies to synthetics. Be sure to look out for clarity, colour and cut, as these factors will determine the final price of your diamond.
